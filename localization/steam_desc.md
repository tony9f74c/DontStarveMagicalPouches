[h1]Features:[/h1]
- Portable containers of various configurable sizes.
- The Icy Magical Pouch holds food and can keep it fresh forever.
- The Magical Pouch holds everything else.
- [b]Auto-Collect[/b]: Pouches can automatically collect items from the ground when you walk over them.

All features are configurable in the mod settings.

[h1]Options[/h1]
- [b]Recipe Tab[/b]: The tab where the recipe is found.
- [b]Recipe Tech[/b]: The building that the recipe requires.
- [b]MP Size[/b]: The number of slots in the Magical Pouch.
- [b]MP Rope, Spider Web, Purple Gems[/b]: The materials that the recipes require.
- [b]Toggle IMP[/b]: Toggles the Icy Magical Pouch recipe on/off.
- [b]IMP Size[/b]: The number of slots in the Icy Magical Pouch.
- [b]IMP Spoilage[/b]: How much longer does the food last in the Icy Pouch.
- [b]IMP Rope, Spider Web, Blue Gems[/b]: The materials for the Icy Magical Pouch.
- [b]Enable Auto-Collect[/b]: Adds a button to the pouches to toggle auto-collection of items off the ground when you step on them.
- [b]Collect Interval[/b]: The auto-collection interval in seconds.
- [b]Sanity/Hunger/Health Drain on Auto-Collect[/b]: Enable/disable stat drain when pouches auto-collect items, and how many items per stat point.
- [b]UI Horizontal/Vertical Position[/b]: UI offset positions.

[h1]Github:[/h1] https://github.com/tony9f74c/DontStarveMagicalPouches

[h1]Notes:[/h1]
- The pouches are crafted at the Shadow Manipulator by default.
- Auto-collect has a sanity cost by default to prevent abuse.
